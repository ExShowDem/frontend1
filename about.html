Objectives

• To recognise outstanding local projects and local organisations for their contribution in the promotion and adoption of digitalisation tools and workflows and in bringing about innovation in these areas to improve productivity, quality, sustainability and safety, with measurable KPIs; and

• To collect the knowledge and experience of the digitalisation leaders and share with the industry through post-award seminars and workshops.
